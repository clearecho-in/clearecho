Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 2, 2026
Last Updated: March 2, 2026

At ClearEcho, we strive to provide high-quality services and products. This policy outlines when refunds are applicable and how they are processed to ensure a fair experience for all users.

01

When Refunds Are Issued

We offer refunds or service credits under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Failures: If a service you paid for is not delivered due to a technical error on our part.
  • Unfulfilled Orders: If a task or order is submitted but cannot be completed within our specified processing window.
  • Duplicate Billing: If you are accidentally charged more than once for the same transaction.
  • Cancellations: If a service is cancelled by our team before any processing or fulfillment has begun.
02

When Refunds Are Not Issued

Refunds are generally not provided in the following cases:

  • Change of Mind: For digital products or subscriptions once the service has been accessed or used.
  • User Error: If a service failure is caused by incorrect user input (e.g., providing an invalid URL or incorrect credentials).
  • Third-Party Interference: If a task is completed successfully but later removed or restricted by a third-party platform (e.g., Reddit, GitHub, or Hosting Providers) beyond our control.
  • Completed Digital Goods: Once a digital asset (like custom art or a downloadable file) has been delivered.
03

Refund Process

  • Credit Returns: For SaaS or tool-based projects, refunds are typically processed as account credits to be used for future services.
  • Original Payment Method: For physical goods or significant service failures, refunds may be issued to your original payment method (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) at our discretion.
  • Processing Time: Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account once approved.
04

Physical Goods

For physical artwork or merchandise:

  • Damaged Items: If an item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photo evidence for a replacement or refund.
  • Returns: Items must be returned in their original condition. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was defective.
05

Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period, and no further charges will be made. We do not offer pro-rated refunds for partial months.
